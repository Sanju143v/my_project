About VaaniVerse

        
Bridging languages and voices with the power of AI

      

      

        

          

          
Our Mission

          
VaaniVerse aims to break language barriers and make voice technology accessible to everyone. We believe
            that every voice matters, and every language deserves to be heard.

        

        

          

          
Our Vision

          
To create a world where language is no longer a barrier ‚Äî where anyone can communicate, create, and express
            themselves freely across all languages and cultures.

        

        

          

          
Technology Stack

          
Built with Node.js, Express, Web Speech API, Web Audio API, and MyMemory Translation API. Features
            multi-layered music generation and AI vocal synthesis.

        

        

          

          
Our Team

          
VaaniVerse is developed as a major college project by a passionate team of students committed to innovation
            and excellence in AI technology.
